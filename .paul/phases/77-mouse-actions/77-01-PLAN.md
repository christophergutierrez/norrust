<objective>
## Goal
Add clickable UI buttons in the sidebar for common gameplay actions: End Turn, Recruit, and Help (?).

## Purpose
Players shouldn't need to memorize keyboard shortcuts for essential actions. Clickable buttons provide immediate discoverability and let mouse-only players participate fully.

## Output
- "End Turn" button in sidebar (visible during gameplay, own turn)
- "Recruit" button in sidebar (visible when on a keep hex with a leader)
- "Help (?)" button in sidebar (always visible)
- Buttons respond to left-click in the sidebar panel area
</objective>

<context>
## Source Files
@norrust_love/main.lua — love.mousepressed handler, sidebar area (rightmost 200px)
@norrust_love/draw.lua — draw_frame, sidebar rendering
</context>

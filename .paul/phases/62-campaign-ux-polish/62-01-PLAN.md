<objective>
## Goal
Add veteran recruitment to the recruit panel and fix the faction picker for preset/campaign scenarios.

## Purpose
Veterans surviving a campaign scenario should be recruitable in the next scenario (shown in the recruit panel alongside normal recruits). The faction picker currently shows for preset scenarios where factions are already determined by the scenario file — this misleads the user.

## Output
- Recruit panel shows veterans (from roster) as additional options above normal recruits
- Veteran placement via recruit panel click on castle hex
- Preset/campaign scenarios skip the faction picker entirely (factions auto-assigned from scenario data)
</objective>
